FRONTIER

A FRamework for Overcoming Natural Threats to Islanded Energy Resilience (FRONTIER)

FRONTIER provides island(ed) communities and island(ed) energy system experts with:

  • An energy resilience investment decision tool specifically designed for island(ed) communities
  • Easy-to-use platform to visualize natural threat scenarios
  • Benefit-Cost analysis tool complemented with other societal metrics
